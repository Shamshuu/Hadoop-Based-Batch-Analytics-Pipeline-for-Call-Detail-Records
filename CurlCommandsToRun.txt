Step 2: Triggering DAGs via curl (Git Bash)
Feature 1: Top Callers by Spend
DAG ID: top_callers_by_spend_dag (associated with top_callers.py)

curl -X POST -u admin:admin -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"conf": {"run_id": "top_callers_run"}}' \
  http://localhost:8080/api/v1/dags/top_callers_by_spend_dag/dagRuns
